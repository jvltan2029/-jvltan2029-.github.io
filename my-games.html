My Games

Greetings!

This is the Game Center! This is a collection of some of the games that I have played and what I consider to be my favorites.

Pokemon

Welcome to the World of Pokemon!

A Pokemon game is a RPG game where the player will travel to catch, train, and defeat creatures called Pokemon

I have played Pokemon games such as, Emerald, Ruby, Sapphire, Diamond, Black, and Go

Minecraft

Minecraft is a 3D sandbox adventure game where many players can play together to explore and build their imaginary world.

Me and my friends have played this multiple times especially during long vacations.

Valorant

Valorant is a team-based, first-person tactical shooter game where player control Agents that have unique abilities to fight the other team.

I rarely play this game but it is still one of my favorites.

Bloons TD 6

Bloons TD 6 is a 3D tower defense strategy game where the player places different kinds of monkeys around the map to prevent balloons (Bloons) from invading their base.

This is the game that I play when trying to pass the time and I have over 30 hours on this game.

Roblox

Roblox is a gaming platform where a person has thousands of games to choose from. It can be RPG, FPS, tycoons, and many more!

I have played Blox Fruits, Grow A Garden, Phantom Forces, and some other games on Roblox.

Call of Duty Mobile (CODM)

Call of Duty Mobile is a free-to-play first-person tactical shooter game that features multiplayer modes.

I played this with some of my friends and it was a great experience.

Mobile Legends Bang Bang (MLBB)

Mobile Legends is a free-to-play 5v5 Multiplayer Online Battle Arena game where teams players controll their heroes to destroy the enemy base.

This is a game that my friends and I have played for quite some time.

Car Parking Multiplayer 1&2 (CPM 1&2)

Car Parking Multiplayer is a free-to-play multiplayer game that features realistic cars that can be driven in the in-game map.

I mostly play this game whenever I am bored or want to do something different.
