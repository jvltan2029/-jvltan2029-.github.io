Oliver / Ollie

This is my pet dog, he is a Jack Russell Terrier named Ollie! He is a very energetic dog and always likes to play with us. He is currently 4 years old and will turn 5 on March 28, 2026. Whenever I am sad or not feeling well, he always manages to cheer me up.
Everytime I get home during the weekends, I always give enough of my time to play with him, since that is the only chance I get to spend time with him. He loves to run around the garage whenever a family member gets home.
