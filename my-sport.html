Badminton.

What is badminton? Badminton isn't just some fun game, its a sport that requires dedication, focus, practice, skill, and talent.

I started playing badminton at a young age, and over the years I have improved a lot.
It taught me the value patience when playing a long exhausting game, resilience whenever I lost, and discipline everytime I trained. Each clear, drive, and smash that I have done served as steps in my growth.

Badminton became a passion, a way for me to push myself further than I ever thought I could.
